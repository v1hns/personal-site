OncoScan AI

Early oral cancer detection powered by computer vision — built with leading cancer researchers in India, incubated as a startup, and scaling across the Indian healthcare market.

📄 Patent Pending ☁️ AWS Credits Awarded

Overview

OncoScan AI is an early-stage startup building a computer vision model for oral cancer detection. Oral cancer has one of the lowest survival rates when caught late — but very high survival rates when detected early. The problem is that early-stage lesions are easy to miss without specialist imaging, and specialists are concentrated in urban centers in India while the disease burden is spread nationally.

As CEO & CTO, I led the technical development of the detection model while simultaneously coordinating a national data collection effort — connecting doctors across India to contribute annotated imaging data for training. The result is a model with real clinical data diversity, built in collaboration with leading cancer researchers.

Data & go-to-market

Coordinated with medical professionals nationally to gather a diverse, clinician-annotated dataset. This physician-network approach both improves model quality and seeds the go-to-market — early contributors become early adopters. The startup was incubated and awarded AWS credits to support cloud infrastructure and model training at scale.
