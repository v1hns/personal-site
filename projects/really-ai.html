Overview

really.ai is a full-stack AI platform that replaces the cold, transactional real estate search with a warm, AI-driven matching experience — inspired by Boardy. Users submit only their phone number. An AI calls them immediately, learns their situation in a 2-minute conversation, and automatically matches them with compatible counterparties in the network.

When a match is found, both parties receive a consent call from the AI. If both agree, each receives the other's phone number directly — no agent middleman, no forms, no friction.

How it works

  1. User drops their phone number on the landing page
  2. VAPI triggers an outbound AI call powered by Groq LLaMA-3.3-70b
  3. The AI collects role, location, budget, timeline, and requirements conversationally
  4. A matching engine scores compatibility across location, budget, and property type
  5. Matched parties receive AI consent calls — if both say yes, intros are exchanged
